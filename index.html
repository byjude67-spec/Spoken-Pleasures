Scarlet usage rules
✦Scarlet #FF2400 — use for fire icons, flame elements, primary passion moments. Max one per layout section.
✦Dark Scarlet #560319 — use in gradient backgrounds alongside Midnight Black for depth. Never on its own as text.
✦Flame Scarlet #CD212A — best for taglines, italic ems, divider lines, and the "Flames of Desire" wordmark in text.
✦Vivid Crimson #CC0033 — luxury CTA buttons as an alternative to Gold. Pair with white text only.
✦Never use scarlet and gold as competing equal-weight elements. Gold leads, scarlet accents — or scarlet leads on a dark bg with gold as the supporting tone.

Body copy — Montserrat
Body text uses Montserrat at weight 300–500, sized at 13–14px with 1.6–1.7 line-height. Clean and modern — it carries the storytelling voice without competing with the dramatic Cinzel Display headlines.

Voice rules
POV
Always second-person. "You", "Your". Never "our members" or "listeners".
Length
Short. Punchy. One idea per sentence. White space is power.
Tone
Warm but seductive. Intimate, never clinical. Confident, never apologetic.
Keywords
Desire · Pleasure · Fantasy · Secret · Yours · Intimate · Play · Unleash
Avoid
Explicit clinical language. Over-explaining. Passive voice. Exclamation marks.
